Welcome to the Official Website of Yool Bulsho Organization! Here, you'll find information about our activities, community programs, and how you can become part of our mission to build an aware, united, and empowered society.
Yool Bulsho is committed to sustainable development that benefits the entire community.

About Us – Yool Bulsho

Yool Bulsho is an independent community-based organization committed to raising public awareness, expanding educational opportunities, strengthening social unity, and promoting environmental care. The organization was founded in response to the urgent need for a dedicated platform to drive sustainable development within the community.


Yool Bulsho believes that community and environment are two interconnected pillars. Therefore, we focus on:

Continuous awareness campaigns on social and environmental issues

Organizing educational programs, capacity-building trainings, and community cleanup activities

Supporting vulnerable groups and advocating for their rights


Creating opportunities for collaboration, community engagement, and meaningful connections

We are a team of passionate youth with vision, ambition, and a shared purpose—to build an inclusive, supportive, and progressive society.


Our mission is to lead the community toward positive change through collaboration, environmental protection, and the cultivation of an active civic spirit.

Our vision is a united, environmentally conscious, and solution-driven society equipped to overcome its challenges.
